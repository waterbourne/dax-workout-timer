## Italian Cuisine - Learnings to Date

### Regional Map
- **North**: Butter, cream, rice, beef, pork, fresh egg pasta, Alpine cheeses
- **Central**: Olive oil + butter mix, tomato sauces, cured meats, both pasta types
- **South**: Olive oil, tomatoes, seafood, spicy, dried pasta, buffalo mozzarella

### Core Techniques Mastered
1. Soffritto (aromatic base)
2. Risotto methodology (toast → broth → mantecatura)
3. Fresh pasta dough (egg + 00 flour)
4. Sauce philosophy: simplicity, quality ingredients

### Key Ingredients Identified
- Tomatoes: San Marzano (gold standard)
- Oils: Extra virgin olive oil (finish), regular (cooking)
- Cheeses: Parmigiano-Reggiano, Pecorino Romano, Mozzarella
- Meats: Guanciale (carbonara), Pancetta, Prosciutto

### Integration with North Indian
- Both value "layering" flavors
- Dough work parallels (pasta ↔ Indian breads)
- Tomato-onion-garlic foundations in both
- Can design fusion or alternating meal plans
