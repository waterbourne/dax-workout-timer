# Platform-Specific Deal Hunting

## eBay

### Search Operators
```
"iPhone 14" -broken -cracked -parts (buy it now)
macbook pro sort:price_asc
"nintendo switch" lot bundle
```

### Best Times to Buy
- Sunday evenings (auction ending, less competition)
- Late night (3-6 AM Pacific)
- Holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas morning)

### Features to Use
- **Watch list:** Monitor items for price drops
- **Best Offer:** Always offer 60-70% of Buy It Now
- **Auction sniping:** Bid in final 5 seconds
- **Saved searches:** Email alerts for new listings

### Fee Structure
- Final value fee: 13.25% (most categories)
- Listing fee: Free for 250 items/month
- PayPal fee: Included in final value fee
- Shipping: You pay or buyer pays

### Red Flags
- Stock photos only (no actual item photos)
- Seller with < 10 feedback
- "As-is, no returns"
- Vague descriptions
- Prices too good to be true

## Facebook Marketplace

### Search Tips
- Use filters: Category, Price, Date Listed
- Search variations: "MacBook", "macbook", "Apple laptop"
- Sort by "Date Listed" to see newest first

### Negotiation Strategy
1. Message within first hour of listing (best deals go fast)
2. Lead with cash offer ready for pickup
3. Bundle items for discount
4. Be ready to walk away

### Safety
- Meet in public places
- Bring friend for high-value items
- Test electronics before paying
- Cash only, no digital payments

### Advantages
- No fees
- Local pickup (no shipping)
- Can inspect before buying
- Room for negotiation

## Craigslist

### Search Syntax
```
craigslist.org/search/sss?query=iphone&sort=date&min_price=100&max_price=400
```

### Best Categories
- For Sale > Electronics
- For Sale > Furniture
- For Sale > Appliances
- For Sale > Sporting Goods
- For Sale > Musical Instruments

### Response Template
```
Hi, I'm interested in the [item] you posted. Is it still available? 
Would you take $[offer]? I can pick up today with cash. Thanks!
```

### Scam Indicators
- Ships to you (Craigslist is local only)
- Wants payment via wire/Zelle/Venmo
- Too-good prices
- Generic photos from Google

## Liquidation.com

### Auction Types
- **Buy Now:** Fixed price, immediate purchase
- **Bid:** Traditional auction format
- **Sealed Bid:** Submit best offer, winner revealed later

### Condition Codes
- **Brand New:** Original packaging, never used
- **Like New:** Opened, tested working
- **Used:** Obvious use but functional
- **Salvage:** Parts/repair only

### Fees
- Buyer's premium: 5-15% depending on auction
- Shipping: Arranged by you or their freight partner
- No sales tax (resale certificate)

## GovDeals.com

### What Sells
- Surplus vehicles
- Office furniture
- Electronics
- Heavy equipment
- Property (land, buildings)

### Process
1. Register and get approved
2. Bid on items (usually 7-14 day auctions)
3. Win and arrange pickup
4. Cashier's check or wire transfer

### Tips
- Government items = no warranty
- Preview inspections recommended
- Pickup deadlines are strict
- Read terms carefully

## EstateSales.org

### Finding Sales
- Search by ZIP code
- View photos of items
- Sign up for email alerts

### Strategy
- Arrive early for best items
- Negotiate heavily on final day
- Bring cash and transport
- Check for hidden gems

## Amazon Warehouse/Returns

### Amazon Warehouse
- Open box items at discount
- Prime shipping
- Return policy intact

### Bulk Returns (via BULQ)
- Amazon customer returns
- Graded condition
- Electronics, clothing, home goods
- Minimum order requirements

## OfferUp

### Characteristics
- Mobile-first
- Lower prices than eBay
- Less competition
- Good for furniture, electronics

### Search Tips
- Use app for best experience
- Filter by distance
- Check seller ratings

## Mercari

### Advantages
- Lower fees than eBay (10% + 2.9% processing)
- Growing user base
- Good for fashion, collectibles

### Strategy
- Watch for underpriced items
- Sellers often don't know true value
- Quick flips possible
