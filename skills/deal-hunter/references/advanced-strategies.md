# Advanced Deal Hunting Strategies

## Bid Sniping

**What it is:** Placing last-second bids to win auctions at lowest price

**Tools:**
- Gixen (free eBay sniper)
- Myibidder
- Manual: Watch auction, bid in final 5 seconds

**Strategy:**
1. Research completed listings for true market value
2. Set max bid at 60-70% of market value
3. Let sniper tool place bid in final seconds
4. Factor in shipping and fees

## Bulk Lot Breakdown

**What it is:** Buying pallets/liquidation lots and reselling individually

**Pros:** Lower per-item cost, steady inventory
**Cons:** Requires storage, some unsellable items, upfront capital

**Viable Categories:**
- Electronics returns (test everything)
- Clothing lots (brand name focus)
- Tools and hardware
- Home goods

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Start with "like new" or "open box" lots
- Avoid "salvage" or "parts" condition until experienced
- Calculate break-even on worst-case scenario
- Have outlet for unsellable items (donate/scrap)

## Cross-Platform Arbitrage

**Buy on:**
- Facebook Marketplace (underpriced, no fees)
- Craigslist (cash deals, negotiation)
- Estate sales (uninformed sellers)

**Sell on:**
- eBay (largest audience, higher prices)
- Amazon (if brand registered)
- Specialized forums (hobby items fetch premium)

**Example:**
- Buy: Vintage receiver on Facebook for $50
- Sell: To audiophile forum for $200
- Time: 1 week
- Margin: 300%

## Seasonal Buying Patterns

**Buy in January:** Holiday returns, gym equipment
**Buy in April:** Tax refund sales, winter sports gear
**Buy in August:** Back-to-school items, summer gear clearance
**Buy in November:** Pre-Black Friday desperation sales

## Negotiation Scripts

**Opening offer (70% of asking):**
"Hi, I'm interested in [item]. Would you take $[offer] for it? I can pick up today."

**Bundle deal:**
"If I take both the [item1] and [item2], would you do $[offer] for everything?"

**Cash today:**
"I have $[offer] cash and can pick up right now. Let me know!"

## Due Diligence Checklist

Before buying:
- [ ] Check sold comps on eBay (last 90 days)
- [ ] Verify authenticity markers
- [ ] Test if possible
- [ ] Check for missing parts
- [ ] Calculate all costs
- [ ] Have exit strategy if it doesn't sell

## Tax Considerations

- Deduct mileage for pickups
- Home office/storage space deduction
- Sales tax permit if required
- Quarterly estimated taxes on profits
